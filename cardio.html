  1. Aerobics

Aerobics is a form of physical exercise that combines rhythmic aerobic exercise with stretching and strength training routines with the goal of improving all elements of fitness (flexibility, muscular strength, and cardio-vascular fitness).

  • Jacobs Ladder

    • High Intensity Aerobic Training. Go through a 2 minute warm-up getting heart rate up to 70% of Maximum HR..Climb for 15-20 minutes at a constant speed. (Speed should be determined by the rate that you can hold while not exceeding 80% of Maximum HR.) Reduce speed and cool down for 3 minutes. Record time and feet climbed.

  • Clumber Mini

    • The Essential mini stepper tones your lower body through a lateral pedal movement that works your lower abdomen, thighs and glutes, and also slims your waist. For a more all-round workout, the Twister mini stepper lets you tone your body from head to toe.
